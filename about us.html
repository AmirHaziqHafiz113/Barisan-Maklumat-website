OUR STORY

Everyone love popcorn – the king of all snack food. Discovered years ago in Malaysia, popcorn with its mystical beauty has captivated people. But popcorn is largely unchanged across the ages. Pop grain is a seed, and when cooked, it just makes some clever stuff.


When snack food is in mind, Popcorn Monsta becomes a famous name. We are currently operating outside of Malaysia and in South-East Asia reach untapped markets. We are proud of the achievements, but we are continually improving by taking advantage of R&D machinery.


Now, Popcorn Monsta has 20 years' experience and knowledge as the leading industry leader. We are also thoroughly familiar with setting up a smooth snack bar for passionate companies. We provide training in order to ensure standardised quality in all areas.
